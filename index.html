Computer of the HMS Grey

Found on an island floating on top of trees which turned out to be a crashed spacecraft in early 2020. The original spacecraft likely is lost, but its story lives on.

Restored and slightly edited by floy in 2024.

This was initially made for a themed build in Minetest with Mesecons and Digiterms on a long-lost server.

Background image credit: ESA/Hubble & NASA, M. Messa

Built from the original source code, bound together with shoestrings and shoddy plain javascript.
